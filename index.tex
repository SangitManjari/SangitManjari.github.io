\mainmatter
The \emph{Sangīt Manjarī} was first published in 1907 under the
patronage of Narendra Lall Khan, the king of Nārājole and Midnapore.
This site presents a careful transliteration of the original Bengali
notation into Roman script, preserving the swara-marks, octave
indicators, and tāl structure of the source.

Compositions are catalogued by rāga. Each page shows the lyrics followed
by a notated table aligned to the relevant tāl cycle.
